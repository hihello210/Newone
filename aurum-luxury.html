03 The Art of Making

Every detail
demands a
lifetime

  1. 01
    Movement Design

    Our engineers design each calibre from scratch. The AUR-1886 took eleven years of development — a movement that reflects our founding year.

  2. 02
    Anglage & Bevelling

    Each component is hand-bevelled and polished under 30× magnification. A single bridge can take eight hours to finish to Côtes de Genève standards.

  3. 03
    Dial Engraving

    Our engravers are among only fourteen master artisans globally who still practise hand-guilloché. Each dial takes up to four weeks.

  4. 04
    COSC Certification

    Every movement completes 16 days of rigorous position and temperature testing. We exceed the official chronometer benchmark by 40%.
